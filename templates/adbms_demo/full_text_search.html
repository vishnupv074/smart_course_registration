Concept: Full-Text Search (tsvector + GIN)

Standard pattern matching (LIKE '%query%') is slow on large datasets because it requires scanning every row. Full-Text Search uses Generalized Inverted Indexes (GIN) and pre-computed tsvector data to perform instant lookups. It also supports linguistics (stemming) and ranking.

Try searching for word stems like 'program' (matches 'programming') or 'teach' (matches 'teacher').
